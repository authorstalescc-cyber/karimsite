My Story

Building at the Intersection of Code, Design & Education

I'm a graduate of the Faculty of Education, Arabic Language Department — a foundation that taught me how to communicate complex ideas with clarity and structure. That pedagogical mindset flows through everything I build.

Over the years, I've expanded my expertise across web development, graphic design, LMS architecture, and corporate training. I manage WordPress sites at scale, build custom plugins, develop applications with Node.js and PHP, and design with the full Adobe Creative Suite.

I've designed complete training programs for organizations like CFI, EL7 Training & Consulting, and Koyfin — and I've built my own LMS from scratch called Scratch LMS. When I design, I don't just make things look good — I make entire systems work beautifully.
